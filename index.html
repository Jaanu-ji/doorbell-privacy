Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 24, 2026

DoorBell ("the app") is a simple notification app that lets you send a doorbell ring to your friends and family. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

When you set up the app, we collect:

  • Display name — the name you enter during setup
  • Phone number — used to identify your account and let friends find you
  • FCM token — a device token used to deliver push notifications to your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send and receive doorbell ring notifications between users
  • To let your friends find and add you by your phone number
  • To deliver push notifications even when the app is closed

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). We do not store your data on any third-party servers beyond Firebase. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your phone number is only used within the app to identify your account — it is never shared externally.

5. Notifications

The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's notification settings.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by uninstalling the app and contacting us at the email below. We will remove your data from our servers within 7 days.

7. Children's Privacy

DoorBell is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
